Beetroot salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredient Quantity
Beetroot 100 g
Carrots 50 g
Onions 25 g
Potatoes 50 g
Mayonnaise 2 tbsp

Steps:

1. Boil potatoes in a uniform peel, wash, and grate.

2. Peel, wash, and grate the remaining ingredients.

3. Add the mayonnaise and move all the ingredients

Condensed milk

  • Time: 9 hours
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredient Quantity
Milk (1.5%) 150 ml
Dry milk (25%) 150 g
Sugar 150 g

Steps:

1. Mix all the ingredients until smooth.

2. Cook in a water bath for 1 hour.

3. Then put in containers and refrigerate for 8 hours.
